Our Story Where History Meets Luxury

A Timeless Retreat in the Heart of England

Nestled within 40 acres of manicured Warwickshire countryside, Kings Court Hotel is a magnificent Tudor manor house dating back to 1642. Just minutes from the birthplace of Shakespeare, our estate offers an unparalleled blend of historic grandeur and contemporary luxury.

Whether you're seeking a romantic escape, a family celebration, or a corporate retreat, our dedicated team ensures every moment at Kings Court is extraordinary.
